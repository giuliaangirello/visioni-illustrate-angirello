Hattie Clark

Hattie Clark's illustrations are a pure reflection of her character - friendly,
kind & enthusiastic. She draws the kind of folks you want to sit down and
have a cup of tea with. Based in Leeds (UK), Hattie has perfected her
self-proclaimed 'wobbly' illustration style and loves to draw a wide variety
of subjects, including people, animals and buildings. She can work both
digitally or by hand, using brush & ink (see if you can spot which is which).
This also makes her work perfect for animation, as you can see throughout
her portfolio.

Some of the people Hattie has worked with so far include: Stagecoach,
Stack Magazines, Pieminister, Lucy and Yak, The Charity for Civil Servants,
Bath Spa University, House of Fu, Leeds Bid. We predict that's a list which
will grow and grow!
